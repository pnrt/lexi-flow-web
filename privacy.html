Privacy Policy for Lexi Flow

Last Updated: December 23, 2025

LexiFlow ("we", "us", or "our") operates the LexiFlow mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information we receive from users of the App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is secure.

Overview

LexiFlow is designed to work primarily offline. The App operates using local storage on your device.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to external servers.

Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information locally on your device:

  • Your learning progress and statistics
  • Words you've studied and mastered
  • App usage patterns and preferences
  • Custom settings and preferences

This information is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

How We Use Information

Any information collected by the App is used solely to:

  • Track your learning progress
  • Provide personalized learning experiences
  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience
  • Remember your preferences and settings

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Android's Room database. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information when you access or enter it. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

LexiFlow does not integrate with any third-party services that collect personal information. The App functions completely offline with the exception of potential in-app purchase functionality through Google Play Billing, which is managed by Google and subject to Google's own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

The App does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to take necessary actions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
